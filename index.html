How carbon-negative chalk works.

01

Capture

Atmospheric CO₂ is captured and mineralised into magnesium carbonate, locking the carbon permanently into the crystal structure.

02

Verify

Every batch is independently tested for purity (≥99%) and carbon balance. Full CoA and SDS documentation available per lot.

03

Climb

The chalk performs like any premium MgCO₃. The difference is what it took from the atmosphere to make it. Every bag is a small piece of captured pollution.
